The Business Plan

November 6, 2013

Some people contact us from time to time asking where is our business plan, if we could share it with them. We’d love to, but the truth is that there is no formal business plan. Not any… at all. I know what you are thinking. And to answer your question, there are three reasons why we didn’t write one.

One, because we set up the lab in 80 days. And that did not give us a lot of time for planning. So we kept improvising.
Two, because the range of possibilities is so big that we can write whatever output we want. Anything between bankrupt and Fortune 500.
And three, the most important, because we are driven by passion.

I’m not saying that you should follow our steps. What I am saying is that in our very particular case it didn't help us. We love machines and calculators, but we are not machines nor calculators. Trust me, we spent our time in more productive tasks. Doing this is not motivated by a financial result. We just feel that we have to do it, because we have faith, because we believe in it, because we think it is correct, because we love what we are doing. And this is it. There is not any other reason.

So what? Do you like our business plan?
