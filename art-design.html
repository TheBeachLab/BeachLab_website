ART AND DESIGN

THE POWER OF PERFECTION

In 2013 I traveled to Kyoto after the annual International Fab Lab Conference. While I was walking towards the bamboo forest I spotted the entrance of Tenryu-ji temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. What I saw made me burst into tears. I couldn't stand so much beauty. That experience was the power of perfection. Perfection is a very powerful message.

VIDEO PRODUCTION

I love producing video for my modest Youtube Channel, where I play the roles of director, scriptwriter, director of photography, actor, lighting and audio technician, set designer, editing... you name it. There is a lot to learn from movies. When I watch a movie, I am not only paying attention to the story, I am carefully examining the framing, the lighting, the music. I like to watch movies multiple times and discover new things, small details that I missed before.

EVENT STREAMING

During the lockdown of the pandemic I had one of those lifetime fast learning experiences. We were notified that the International Conference Fab 16 would not happen in Montreal and within 2 months we had to learn how to produce the alternative live event Fabx Live. I learned Open Broadcaster Software (aka OBS) which is a software for live streaming and video recording. I filled up my room with monitors. I created my own hardware and software solution for live scene control. It was crazy. It was exhausting. I drank more coffe that would probably be advisable. But we created one of the most amazing live events ever, built from the ground up, with no previous experience. It was also an incredible remote team syncronyzation with lots of mistakes and funny moments (note: they are only funny when we remember them, not while they were happening).
