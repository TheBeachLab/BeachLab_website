LIFE AND WANDER

WHO AM I

I like to define myself as a Civil Engineer 'with feelings'. I am temporarily walking the journey of life like a shooting star in slow motion. And to answer your question, my name is Fran Sanchez, I am the Spirit behind The Beach Lab.

THIRST FOR LEARNING

Measured by traditional education standards, I could probably be tagged one of the worst -if not the worst- student in my University. Which is kind of interesting, because I love learning. I used to skip class to run to the library. I was debouring books, any kind of book. The more I read, the more I want to know. It's a sense of urgency. So much to learn, so little time. I never start a book from the first page, I open a random one. Sometimes I read backwards, sometimes I scan words jumping across chapters, sometimes I jump into another book. That's is how I ended up doing so different and apparently unrelated things, From Ocean Yatchmaster, to engineering, to music and arts, to entepreneurship and many more to come. And yet, I could probably be one of the worst student in my University.

LOVE FOR NATURE

There's something about this planet that produces a strong feeling on me. It goes beyond any logic fascination. It is some sort of energy encoded in every organic and inorganic matter. Even now I can feel it. It's in every drop of the seas, in every dust particle of a mountain, in the air we breathe. It's in what we can see and it's in what we can't. It's everywhere, and I love it. I love this planet and I will stand for it. I am not giving up on Earth, I am not looking forward to go to Mars. I will use all my intelligence, all my skills, all my forces, all the energy I borrowed, to preserve, improve and leave this planet a better place than I found it. I will transmit my sons the passion I have for nature so the cycle will continue. That is my manifesto.

CURIOSITY FOR CULTURES

Blend like an Octopus. Flow like the Wind. Absorb like a Sponge. I am lucky enough to have a job that allows me to experience very diverse and rich cultures across the World. For me it's so important to understand and embrace other people's cultures. It's not only about the treasure of wisdom, but also encounter new forms of intelligence. Intelligence adopts many formats, sometimes very subtle. On the dark side, neglecting other cultural beings lead to missunderstandings, fear and hate. The perfect ingredients for people's manipulation and one of our modern societies sins. Cultural awareness balances three fundamental concepts. Identity has an inbound attractor that seeks the uniqueness of the group or invididual. Diversity, on the other side, is an outbound force that repels from any other. A thirt element, Complementarity, gives balance and harmony to the whole. The beauty of multiculturalism is that you don't need to abandon your own culture to love, appreaciate and respect other people's cultures.
